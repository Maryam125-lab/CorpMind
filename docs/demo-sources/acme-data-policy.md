# Acme Data Protection Policy

Acme requires confidential customer and employee data to be encrypted at rest and in transit. Access reviews must be completed every quarter by the security and compliance owners.

All incidents involving confidential information must be escalated to Security within 24 hours. The incident owner must document the timeline, impacted systems, containment steps, and final remediation evidence.

The policy requires audit evidence to be retained for two years. Missing evidence, delayed escalation, or unauthorized data access may create compliance risk and must be reviewed by management.
