# Vendor Service Agreement

The vendor must maintain 99.5 percent monthly uptime for the document processing service. Priority incidents require a response within four business hours and a written root cause analysis within five business days.

Either party may terminate the agreement after a material breach if the breach remains uncured for 30 days. The vendor has liability for service interruptions caused by negligence, security failure, or failure to meet agreed compliance obligations.

Payment milestones depend on successful delivery, acceptance testing, and evidence that support procedures are operating as expected.
